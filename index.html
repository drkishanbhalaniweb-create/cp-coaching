No. We never speak to examiners or the VA on your behalf. Our role is to coach and educate you so you can speak for yourself clearly and honestly in the exam.

No. We do not provide legal advice or represent you before the VA. For legal questions or representation, please contact an accredited representative or attorney.

No one ethically can. We cannot control who your examiner is or how the VA decides your claim. We focus on helping you feel more prepared and able to communicate your experience clearly.
