Frequently asked questions

Do you talk to the examiner or VA for me?

No. We never speak to examiners or the VA on your behalf. Our role is to coach and educate you so you can speak for yourself clearly and honestly in the exam.

Is this legal advice or representation?

No. We do not provide legal advice or represent you before the VA. For legal questions or representation, please contact an accredited representative or attorney.

Can you guarantee my exam will go well or my claim will be approved?

No one ethically can. We cannot control who your examiner is or how the VA decides your claim. We focus on helping you feel more prepared and able to communicate your experience clearly.

Ideally within 1‚Äì3 weeks of your exam date so the information feels fresh. If your exam is sooner than that, a focused session can still help you feel more grounded.

Any exam notices, decision letters, or notes you've made about your symptoms and limitations. If you don't have everything organized yet, that's okay‚Äîwe'll work with what you have.
